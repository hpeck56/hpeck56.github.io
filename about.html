Work About Resume


Hi, my name is Henry Peck.

 Also known as Hen to my friends, Pecker to my coaches, rooster to my campers (see picture on the left), bubby to my grandparents, and hennyp to my Xbox opponents.

Despite the many names I have today, doctors used to know me as the child who would lose his sight. Because of a rare optical condition I developed at birth, my family was told that I would never see well enough to drive a car. While there were many who believed that nothing could be done, the genius of a few surgeons and engineers yielded implants which, through several procedures, addressed my condition. Fortunately the experience left minimal physical scarring, but it gave me a profound sense of gratitude and a vision (pun intended) to improve the quality of life for others with unique medical challenges through design and technology.

Outside of engineering I love to teach, work with young people, fanatically watch NHL hockey, taste whiskeys and borbouns, and play old Nintendo video games.
