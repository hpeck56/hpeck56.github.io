MedHacks Hack-a-thon
Hosted at Johns Hopkins University's Medical Campus, MedHacks is a three day hack-a-thon uniting students and industry professionals with a passion for improving healthcare. The competition features three tracks for innovation; the three tracks from MedHacks 2017 were Medication Adherence with CVS Health, Patient Safety & Quality with Hopkins Medicine, and Access to Care with Blue Cross Blue Shield.
